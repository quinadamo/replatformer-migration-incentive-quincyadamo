Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How does 1-click migration actually work? +
We scan your Squarespace site, extract products (including photos, descriptions, and prices), pull your brand assets (logo, color palette, fonts), and rebuild your store on Shopify using our Horizon theme. It happens automatically, and you see a working preview before you sign up. Your existing Squarespace site keeps running the whole time.
Will I lose my SEO rankings? +
No. We auto-generate 301 redirects for every Squarespace URL that has traffic, so Google keeps crediting your new Shopify pages for the old rankings. Sitemap is regenerated automatically.
What happens to my custom domain? +
Transferring your domain is one click once you're in admin. Your yoursite.com stays live throughout, zero downtime. We give you step-by-step instructions, or let Sidekick (our AI assistant) handle the DNS for you. Your $20 domain credit covers the transfer fee.
What doesn't migrate automatically? +
Subscriptions, memberships, and any Squarespace-specific integrations (like Acuity booking) don't migrate directly, since the data models are different. We flag these in your migration checklist so nothing surprises you after launch. Sidekick can help you set up Shopify equivalents.
How do fees differ between Squarespace and Shopify? +
With this offer, your first year is $93 total on Shopify ($1/mo × 3, then $10/mo × 9) vs $324 on Squarespace Commerce. Plus you get 1% back on Shopify Payments sales (up to $15,000 in year-one credits) and a $20 domain credit. Shopify Payments has similar processing fees to Stripe, with slightly lower rates on higher plans.
How long does a full migration take, end to end? +
The preview is built for you automatically. After you sign up, domain transfer takes ~6 hours to propagate DNS. Most Squarespace merchants are fully live on Shopify within 24 hours of starting the migration. Migrations using the manual CSV route typically take 2-4 hours of your time. Hiring a partner takes 1-3 weeks depending on complexity.
